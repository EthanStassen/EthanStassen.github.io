The first step to making maple syrup is to tap
trees. You need to get some buckets and spiles,
then find a maple tree. Next, you drill a small
hole in the tree and place the spile in the tree
and hang the bucket on it.

The second step is to collect your sap once the
buckets are somewhat full. Make sure to filter it
into a bigger bucket for storage.

The third step is to boil the sap. This takes
a while, but it evaporates all the water from
the sap and makes it into actual syrup.

The final step once you are done boiling is to
store or eat your syrup! Syrup is best stored in
glass jars or jugs.
